## Full description
Comic Creator helps you make comics, manga strips, and storyboards on your Android device — no account required.

**Easy to start**
• Pick a layout template (4-panel grid, webtoon scroll, comic strip, and more)
• Tap panels to add text, speech bubbles, photos, and drawings
• Preview every page before you share

**Made for beginners**
• Automatic save — your work stays on this device
• Simple tools: backgrounds, upload, characters, bubbles, text, draw
• Export to PDF and share anywhere

**Privacy first**
• Projects stored locally on your phone
• No sign-up, no cloud upload of your comics

Perfect for hobbyists, students, and anyone trying comic-making for the first time.
