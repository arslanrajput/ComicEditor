# Privacy Policy — Comic Creator

**Last updated:** June 2026

Comic Creator ("the app") is a local comic editing tool. Your projects are stored on your device.

## Data we collect

We do **not** collect personal information, analytics tied to your identity, or comic content on our servers.

## Data stored on your device

- Comic project names and page layouts
- Panel content (text, drawings, imported images)
- App preferences (e.g. tutorial completed)

## Permissions

| Permission | Why |
|------------|-----|
| Photos / Camera | Only when you choose Upload to add an image to a panel |
| Internet | May be used by the system PDF share/print dialog |

## Deleting your data

Delete individual projects in the app, or uninstall the app to remove all local data.

## Children

The app does not knowingly collect data from children. Content is created by the user on device.
